A taste of Japan in NZ...

Nakano BC, from Wakayama in Japan, produce award-winning sake, umeshu (plum wine) and spirits. For over 30 years, Evergreen Export has been proud to distribute Nakano's products in NZ.

As a boutique producer, Nakano prides itself on its artisanal products, while drawing on Wakayama's rich heritage and beautiful produce.
